Street School

Pehchaan started its street school Education project for the Tribal, poor and orphans at Bihar, Delhi Mumbai. The School initiated in the year 2014 and there are approximately 85 students are receiving quality education as this facility.

Medical Camp

Pehchaan is working at the grassroots level towards building healthy communities which would meet the achievement of the Millennium Development Goals as well as contribute to poverty reduction. Through the support of the donors, they have been implementing projects to cater to children’s various needs, making their childhood happier and more fulfilling. They are working relentlessly to give children a chance at building a brighter future and in this quest, every bit of support counts.

Sports

To help young women realize the ideals of womanhood and prepare themselves for the responsibilities in the home, the nation and the global community, Pehchaan have their sports facility. Women Sports provide a platform that gives courage and confidence, and lays the foundation of women of strong characters who can be a productive part of the society. They strive to support the girls from financially disadvantaged background to tap their undiscovered potential and become world-class sportspersons.
